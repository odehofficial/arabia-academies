Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026

1. Who we are

Arabia Academies ("we", "us") operates this website, which presents our online educational communities (eCom Arabia +, Zambeel Dropshipping, Iraq e-Com, Ai Arabia, ENE X eCom). You can contact us any time at odeh.ecom@gmail.com or via WhatsApp.

2. Information we collect

  • Information you give us — when you message us on WhatsApp, Instagram or email (for example your name, phone number and what you write to us).
  • Usage data — basic technical data such as browser type, device, pages visited and approximate region, collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Enrollment data — when you join an academy, your registration and payment are handled by Skool (or Instagram for ENE X eCom); we receive your membership details from those platforms.

3. How we use it

To respond to your messages, provide and improve our academies and this website, understand which content is useful, and send information you asked for. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Cookies & Meta Pixel

This site uses a small number of cookies and similar browser-storage technologies to remember your language choice and understand how the site is used. We also use the Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram) to measure visits and the effectiveness of our advertising; it may set cookies and share usage data with Meta Platforms. In regions where consent is required, the Pixel only runs after you accept the cookie notice. You can delete or block cookies in your browser settings at any time; the site will still work. See Meta's Privacy Policy for how Meta processes data.

5. Third-party services

Parts of the experience are provided by third parties with their own privacy policies: Skool (community hosting and payments), Google Fonts, WhatsApp, Meta platforms (Instagram, Facebook, Meta Pixel), YouTube, TikTok, X, LinkedIn, Zambeel and Exporta. When you follow a link to these services, their policies apply.

6. Data retention

We keep messages and correspondence only as long as needed to serve you and meet legal obligations, then delete or anonymize them.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under the EU GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. Email us at odeh.ecom@gmail.com and we will respond.

8. Children

Our services are not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the latest version will always be on this page with its effective date.
